About CareSync

CareSync Hospital is the largest multispecialty hospital in Tanjore. We extend the traditional Indian hospitality to Indian and international patients, combining it with our cutting-edge technology. For more than 10 years, CareSync Hospital, Thanjavur, has been a household name in the Delta region of Tamil Nadu for providing world-class healthcare at affordable cost.

Our healthcare model is structured to meet the rising challenges in the field of medicine and offers comprehensive care to patients. We offer the widest and most comprehensive range of outpatient and inpatient services to ensure a wholesome patient experience.
